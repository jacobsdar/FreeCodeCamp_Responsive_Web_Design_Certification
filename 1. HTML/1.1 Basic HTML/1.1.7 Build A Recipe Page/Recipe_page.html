Slice of Golden Toast

Simple, yet universal, versatile meal for any time of the day. Easy to follow, easy to master. Minimal ingredients and kitchenware needed. Dig in.

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of bread (or 2 or 3, depends on how hungry you are)
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Optional :
  • 1 tablespoon jam (to go with your tea)
  • 1 tablespoon peanutbutter (for all you nutters)
  • 1 squirt of honey (Honey, honey, don't conceal it, ah-hah, honey, honey)
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter(for the slow pokes)

Instructions

  1. Grab toaster
  2. Put it down on a flat surface, preferably a kitchen counter
  3. Plug it in, don't get electrocuted now
  4. Turn plug on
  5. Grab a slice of bread, white, brown, heck, pink if you can
  6. Pop it in the slot in the toaster
  7. Push the knob down, down goes the bread
  8. You remembered to adjust the number knob thing to golden brown?
  9. No...no matter, don a pair of safety goggles and make sure that bread toasts golden brown
  10. Once it reaches a perfect golden hit the eject button
  11. Be sure to grab those rocketing, perfect, golden square discs
  12. Almost done now, take a butter knife, and apply an ample buttery base
  13. Eat or optionally...spread preferred topping on toast. Rinse, repeat and eat
